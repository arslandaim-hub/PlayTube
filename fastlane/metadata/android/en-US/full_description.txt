PlayTube is a fast, private, and feature-rich YouTube client for Android.

Key Features:
- Background Play: Keep listening to music or podcasts while using other apps.
- Picture-in-Picture: Watch videos in a floating window.
- Downloads: Save videos or audio for offline use.
- Subscription Management: Stay updated with your favorite creators without an account.
- Search History & Privacy: Control your data with options to pause history.
- Dynamic UI: Smooth, full-screen browsing experience.
- Open Source: Built for the community, with no tracking or non-free SDKs.

Enjoy a premium video experience for free.
